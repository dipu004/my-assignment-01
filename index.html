Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation, for its part, can refer to something new
or to a change made to an existing product, idea, or field.

Sustainability

In addition to natural resources, we
also need social and economic resources.
As a civilization, we are concerned about the sustainability of our planet.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology,
promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy,
digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.
